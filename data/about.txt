This is a game created by the SBUHacks Team. At the beginning of the game, you will be given a target. Your goal is to control the character to shift left and right using arrow keys to collect all foods/sports that are necessary to accomplish the goal. Each kind of food will add certain amount of calories to the current calories. Each kind of sport will decrease the current calories by certain amounts. Binge-eating or under-eating will end the game.
